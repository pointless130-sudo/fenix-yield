Pricing FAQ

How does billing work?

You pay once for your chosen plan length (1, 3, 6, or 12 months). There is no automatic recurring charge — you will receive a renewal reminder with a new payment link before your plan expires.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Simply don't renew when your plan expires. No cancellation fees, no lock-in.

Can I switch tiers or plan lengths?

Yes — on renewal you can switch to any tier or plan length you like.

What happens when my plan expires?

You'll receive a reminder 7 days before expiry with a renewal link. If you don't renew, automation pauses and you revert to Scout (free). Your Safe wallet and funds are completely unaffected.

Do coupon codes work on all plans?

Yes — coupon codes apply a fixed dollar discount to whichever tier and plan length you choose.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day refund on your first payment if the software does not function as described. See our Refund Policy.
