Common questions

Pricing FAQ

When am I billed?

Subscriptions are billed monthly on the date you sign up. Your first billing cycle begins immediately upon purchase.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel at any time and you retain access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.

Can I switch tiers?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately (prorated). Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day refund on your first payment if the software does not function as described. See our Refund Policy for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via our payment processor. Payments are processed securely — we do not store card details.

Is there a free trial?

The Scout tier is permanently free with no time limit. It gives you full access to APY tracking, alerts, and reports before committing to a paid plan.

What does Fenix Yield actually do?

Fenix Yield is a software automation tool. It monitors DeFi protocol APIs, identifies the highest available yield, and proposes or executes transactions to your self-custodial Safe wallet. It does not hold, manage, or have access to your funds at any time.
