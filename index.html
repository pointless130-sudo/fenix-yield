How Fenix Yield works

Set it up once in 20 minutes. The bot handles everything daily — finding yield, proposing transactions, and compounding rewards. You just approve with 1–3 tap approval in Safe Mobile depending on protocol.

1

Create your Safe wallet

Set up a non-custodial Safe multi-sig wallet at app.safe.global. Your funds stay under your control at all times.

2

Register with the bot

Send /start on Telegram, enter your invite code, and connect your Safe address. Takes under 2 minutes.

3

Bot finds best yield daily

Every 24 hours the bot compares APYs across all protocols and routes your ETH to the highest return automatically.

4

Approve with one tap

Get a push notification in Safe Mobile. Review the transaction details and approve with a single tap. Nothing executes without you.
