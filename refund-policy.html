Our Refund Policy

✓ Eligible 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you subscribe to Optimizer or Autopilot and the Service does not function as described on our website within the first 7 days of your initial purchase, you are entitled to a full refund of your first payment. Contact us at support@fenix-yield.com within 7 days of your purchase date with your Telegram username and a description of the issue.

✗ Not Eligible Renewal Payments

Renewal payments are non-refundable. When you choose to renew your plan, you are purchasing access for the full selected period (1, 3, 6, or 12 months). We do not issue partial refunds for unused time within a purchased period.

✗ Not Eligible Investment Losses

Fenix Yield is a software automation tool. We are not responsible for and do not refund losses arising from DeFi protocol performance, market volatility, smart contract exploits, gas fees, or any other on-chain outcomes. These risks are disclosed in our Terms of Service.

✗ Not Eligible Change of Mind After 7 Days

Refunds are not issued for change of mind, dissatisfaction with DeFi yields, or decisions to stop using the Service after the 7-day guarantee window. Simply do not renew when your plan expires.

How to Cancel

Fenix Yield does not use automatic recurring billing. Your plan is active for the period you purchased (1, 3, 6, or 12 months). To stop future charges, simply do not renew when you receive your renewal reminder.

If you would like to deactivate your account before your plan expires, email support@fenix-yield.com with your Telegram username. You will retain access until your current period ends.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days and returned to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or card issuer.

We reserve the right to deny refund requests that show evidence of abuse (e.g., repeated refund requests across multiple accounts).

Scout Tier

The Scout tier is permanently free. No payment is required and no refunds are applicable.

Questions

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or wish to request a refund, please contact us at support@fenix-yield.com. We aim to respond to all support requests within 1 business day.

Need help before cancelling?

Most issues can be resolved quickly. Reach out and we'll do our best to get you set up correctly.
