Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 27, 2026

Summary: We collect only what we need to run the Service — your Telegram ID, Safe wallet address, email (for paid tiers), and subscription status. We do not sell your data. We never have access to your private keys or funds.

1. Who We Are

Fenix Yield is a software automation service operated by its sole owner based in Texas, United States. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Telegram bot (@fenix_yield_bot), website (fenix-yield.com), and related services.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@fenix-yield.com.

2. Information We Collect

Data Source Purpose
Telegram user ID & username Telegram API when you start the bot Account identification, sending notifications
Your name You provide during onboarding Personalizing bot messages
Gnosis Safe wallet address You provide during onboarding Querying on-chain balances, proposing transactions
Email address You provide (paid tiers only) Subscription verification, transactional emails
Subscription tier & status Payment processor webhook Determining feature access
Stake amount preference You provide during onboarding Configuring automation parameters
Transaction history Public blockchain data P&L reporting, harvest tracking
Website analytics Cloudflare (aggregated) Understanding site traffic

We do not collect: private keys, seed phrases, passwords, payment card details, government ID, or any information about your other wallets or holdings outside your registered Safe address.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and deliver the Service (APY tracking, transaction proposals, alerts)
  • Verify subscription status and enforce tier-based access controls
  • Send transactional notifications via Telegram (yield reports, risk alerts, harvest confirmations)
  • Send transactional emails via Resend (subscription confirmation, invite codes)
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent abuse or fraudulent use
  • Improve the Service

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the Service.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a SQLite database on a private VPS server. We implement reasonable technical safeguards including encrypted connections, access controls, and regular backups.

Your Safe wallet address is a public blockchain address. On-chain data (balances, transactions) is public by nature and not subject to our control.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, subject to any legal retention obligations.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Fenix Yield:

  • Telegram — bot infrastructure and message delivery. Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — payment processing. We do not store card details. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.
  • Cloudflare — DNS, CDN, and tunnel infrastructure.
  • Alchemy / public RPC — Ethereum node access for reading on-chain data.
  • Gnosis Safe — transaction proposal API.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

6. Blockchain Data

Transactions proposed or executed by Fenix Yield are recorded on the Ethereum blockchain, which is public and permanent. This includes your Safe wallet address and transaction details. We have no ability to delete or modify blockchain records.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@fenix-yield.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. We use sessionStorage (not persistent cookies) to track referral parameters within a single browsing session. No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies are used.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the Telegram bot or email. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at support@fenix-yield.com.
