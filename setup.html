Step 2

Get ETH into MetaMask

You need ETH in your MetaMask wallet before creating your Safe. This is what the bot will stake to earn yield.

  • 1
    Open MetaMaskTap "Buy" or "Receive" to add ETH
  • 2
    Buy ETH via card or bankUse the built-in buy flow (Transak, MoonPay) or transfer from an exchange like Coinbase
  • 3
    Get at least 0.1 ETH0.05 ETH for staking + a little extra for gas fees (~$0.50 worth)
  • 4
    Make sure you're on Ethereum MainnetCheck the network dropdown in MetaMask — should say "Ethereum", not a testnet
⚠️ Never send ETH directly to your Safe from an exchange. Always send to MetaMask first, then transfer to your Safe in Step 4.
✓

✓

Step 3

Create your Safe wallet

Your Safe is a smart contract wallet that holds your funds. The bot proposes transactions to it — you always stay in control.

  • 1
    Go to app.safe.globalOpen in your phone's browser or on desktop
  • 2
    Click "Create new Safe"Connect your MetaMask when prompted
  • 3
    Keep default settingsYour MetaMask address is already the owner. Threshold stays at 1 of 1.
  • 4
    Confirm in MetaMaskA small gas fee applies (usually under $0.50). Wait ~30 seconds.
  • 5
    Save your Safe addressStarts with 0x and is different from your MetaMask address. Copy and save it.
🛡️ Your Safe is non-custodial — only you can move funds. The bot can only propose transactions, never execute them without your approval.

Step 7 — Autopilot Only

Enable Zodiac auto-execution

Zodiac is a smart contract module that lets the bot execute whitelisted yield transactions automatically — without Safe Mobile approval. You stay in full control: the bot can only call exact yield protocol functions you whitelist.

🚀 This is what makes Autopilot fully hands-free. Once enabled, the bot executes yield transactions, Convex harvests, and compound cycles automatically at any time — no phone needed.
⚠️ This step is done inside the Telegram bot after you register. Complete registration first (next step), then send /whitelist to the bot to begin Zodiac setup.
  • 1
    Register with the bot firstComplete the /start flow in Telegram (next step)
  • 2
    Send /whitelist to the botIt guides you through deploying the Zodiac Roles Module to your Safe
  • 3
    Deploy the module (~$2 gas)One-time transaction that installs the smart contract on your Safe
  • 4
    Grant the bot its roleOne transaction gives the bot permission to call whitelisted functions only
  • 5
    Whitelist the yield functionsBot walks you through approving each protocol function (Lido, Curve, Aave, Convex)
  • 6
    Confirm with /whitelist statusBot confirms "Zodiac: ON" and auto-execution begins immediately
✅ After Zodiac setup the bot will show Zodiac: ON in its logs and execute transactions directly. You can disable anytime with /whitelist disable.

Bot commands

Send any of these in Telegram at any time. Commands marked 🚀 require Autopilot.

🔍 Scout tier: You have access to monitoring commands only. Staking commands require Optimizer or Autopilot.

Quick balance checkYour Safe ETH balance and any LP tokens — available to all tiers

Full portfolioAll positions across all 4 protocols with current USD values — available to all tiers

Yield tracking & P&LTotal deposited, yield earned, annualized APY — available to all tiers
/status
Pending transactionsAny Safe transactions waiting for your signature — available to all tiers

Run yield cycle now ⚡🚀Manually trigger a stake instead of waiting for the daily cycle. Optimizer: proposes to Safe. Autopilot: executes directly.
/harvest
Claim Convex rewards ⚡🚀Checks claimable CRV + CVX. If above $10, proposes (or executes) claim and swap back to ETH.
/setstake
Change stake amount ⚡🚀Update how much ETH gets staked each daily cycle. Minimum 0.05 ETH.
/setprotocol
Control protocols ⚡🚀Pin to a specific protocol, exclude ones you don't want, or return to auto best-APY mode.
/pause / /resume
Pause the bot ⚡🚀Stops the daily cycle for your account. Existing pending transactions are unaffected.
/unstake
Withdraw funds ⚡🚀Step-by-step instructions to exit each protocol with direct links to withdrawal pages.
/whitelist
Zodiac auto-execution 🚀Deploy and manage the Zodiac Roles Module. /whitelist setup to enable, /whitelist status to check, /whitelist disable to turn off.
