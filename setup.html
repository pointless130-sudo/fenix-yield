Step 2 of 10

Set up MetaMask

MetaMask is your Ethereum wallet. It signs transactions — nothing moves without your fingerprint or PIN.

MetaMask — Create wallet
  • 1
    Open MetaMaskTap "Create a new wallet" on the welcome screen
  • 2
    Set a passwordChoose a strong password or enable biometrics
  • 3
    Write down your seed phraseYou'll see 12 words. Write them on paper and store somewhere safe. This is the ONLY way to recover your wallet.
  • 4
    Copy your wallet addressTap your account name at the top — your address starts with 0x. Save it.
⚠️ Never share your 12-word seed phrase with anyone — not the admin, not this bot, not anyone. Anyone who has it can take all your funds.

Step 3 of 10

Buy ETH

You need ETH to stake. Buy it on an exchange and send it to your MetaMask wallet.

Exchange → MetaMask transfer
  • 1
    Create account on an exchangeCoinbase (coinbase.com) is easiest for beginners. Binance and Kraken also work.
  • 2
    Verify your identityExchanges require ID verification. This takes 5-30 minutes.
  • 3
    Buy ETHBuy at least 0.05 ETH. This covers staking plus gas fees (under $0.10 each).
  • 4
    Withdraw to MetaMaskIn the exchange, click Withdraw → Crypto → ETH. Paste your MetaMask address. Select Ethereum network (not BNB or Polygon).
⚠️ Always select Ethereum (ERC-20) network when withdrawing. Sending on the wrong network will lose your ETH.

Step 4 of 10

Create your Safe wallet

Your Safe is a smart contract wallet. It holds your funds and requires your approval before any transaction executes.

app.safe.global — Create Safe
  • 1
    Go to app.safe.globalOpen in your phone's browser or on desktop
  • 2
    Click "Create new Safe"Connect your MetaMask when prompted
  • 3
    Keep default settingsYour MetaMask address is already the owner. Threshold stays at 1 of 1.
  • 4
    Click Create and confirm in MetaMaskA small gas fee applies (usually under $0.50). Wait ~30 seconds.
  • 5
    Save your Safe addressYour Safe address appears after creation. Starts with 0x. Save it — this is different from your MetaMask address.
